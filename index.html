Frequently asked questions

I’ve just bought a new subscription and can’t login
Unfortunately, it can take up to 48 hours for a new subscription to be set up. If you ordered more than 48 hours ago and you are still having trouble logging in, please call us on +44 (0)330 333 1122. Lines open 8:00am - 6.00pm Monday to Saturday (UK time). You can also raise a support query here.
Only one of my subscriptions is showing up in my account
If you've bought more than one subscription and only one is showing up in your account, it may be you bought them using different e-mail address or delivery details. Please contact customer services directly who will be happy to help you.
Can I speak to Customer Services directly?
Please call us on +44 (0)330 333 1122. Lines open 8:00am - 6.00pm Monday to Saturday (UK time). You can also raise a support query here.
I still can’t login. Who can help me?
Yes. Please contact our Customer Services team on +44 (0)330 333 1122. Lines open 8:00am - 6.00pm Monday to Saturday (UK time). You can also raise a support query here.
