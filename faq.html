Frequently Asked Questions

Where can I find more information on other TI Media Limited magazines?

If you want to find out more about the other magazines published by TI Media Limited, please visit www.magazinesdirect.com, where you will find information about all our publications and browse the latest offers.

Why renew my subscription?

If you renew your subscription, you can continue to enjoy having the magazine delivered to your door and you will never have to worry about it selling out at your local news agents. You will also be protected against price increases for at least 12 months. In addition, if you renew by Direct Debit, you will never have to worry about renewing again whilst staying in complete control of your subscription.

When should I renew my subscription? Will I miss any issues?

If you renew your subscription before the date highlighted on the renew your subscription page, your subscription will continue without interruption and you will not miss any issues. If you renew after this date we cannot guarantee this.

What is included in the renewal price?

Prices displayed include delivery costs and taxes (where applicable). Any discounts to the full annual subscription rate will also be explained.

How does Direct Debit work?

UK customers have the option to subscribe by Direct Debit. Depending on the magazine, your payments will be spread into easy, manageable 3, 6 or 12 monthly payments. As Direct Debit is a continuous payment method you are in complete control of your subscription as well as eliminating the need for renewal notices.

How does a continuous credit card work?

Some promotions offer continuous credit card, or automatic renewal. Before the start of each new term, we'll simply charge your credit/debit card with the agreed payment amount. If there are any changes to the payment amount, before we charge your card we will send you a notification of this showing the new subscriber rate at that time, with a number to call and details of the online cancellation facility should you wish to cancel. As this is a continuous payment method you are in complete control of your subscription as well as eliminating the need for renewal notices.

Is the online payment system secure?

Yes. We use special SSL software to encrypt your personal and payment details to ensure that your information is kept secure at all times.

How do you acknowledge the changes I make to my account online?

We will acknowledge any action you perform on the site by means of a confirmation page which will summarise the details of your request. We will also send you a confirmation email with these details for your files. Should you not receive the acknowledgement email or have any questions, please contact our Customer Care Team.

What can I do if I do not receive an issue on time?

Please contact our Customer Care Team . Please remember to notify our Customer Care Team if you change your address.

Why can I claim only one replacement issue online?

We want to ensure that we have your delivery details correct in our database, which is why we ask you to contact our Customer Care Team when you have previously claimed non-delivery of an issue. Our agents can go through your details with you and make any adjustments if necessary.

How do I purchase a single issue or a back issue?

If you wish to purchase a back issue of an Time Inc (UK) magazine you can order online. Alternatively, please call +44 (0)1733 688 964. Lines are open 24 hours a day, seven days a week.

How long does it take for my address change to take effect?

Please allow up to 7 days for any address change to be updated on your online account. The change of address form will show from which issue the magazines will be sent to your new address.

Why can I not change my delivery country online?

The subscription price includes the delivery charges, which varies from country to country. We therefore may have to adjust the amount of issues remaining on your subscription to reflect this. Our customer services agents can help you with this and can make sure your subscription is sent to your new address without any interruption.

How do I cancel my subscription?

If your subscription has a minimum term of 12 months, you can only cancel your subscription at the end of this term.

At the end of this minimum term, and for any subscription that does not have a minimum term, you can cancel by notifying our Customer Care Team.

What do you do with my personal information?

We use the personal information you provide (e.g. name, address, email address, credit card details) to process your subscription order. On the on-line subscriptions order form we will ask you about receiving marketing about our magazines and services and/or those of third parties which may be of interest to you. If you have any questions about this please send an email to dataprotection@timeinc.com.

Do you use "Cookies"?

From time to time we may store "cookies" (small files transmitted between server software and the browser on your computer) on your computer's hard drive to make your use of the on-line subscription process easier. The personal information you provide (e.g. name, address, email address, credit card details) is stored on our secure server, not in a cookie.

How can I save and/or print a copy of the Subscription Terms and Conditions?

We recommend that for your future reference purposes you save and/or print a copy of these documents together with a copy of the order acknowledgement email. We will retain a copy of your order form for the period of your subscription. To print the Subscriptions Terms and Conditions, simply visit the relevant page and choose the print option in your browser.

Help Contacts

You can call, email or write to our Customer Care Team and they will do their utmost to answer any query you may have as efficiently as possible. Emails will be responded to within 48 hours.
