How can we help?

Change of personal details
What do I need to do if I move house?

The quickest way to update your address details is by logging into your account. Alternatively, fill in the form below and one of our Customer Care team will look into this for you.

How long does it take for my address change to take effect?

Please allow up to 7 days for any address change to be updated on your online account. The change of address form will show from which issue the magazines will be sent to your new address.

Why can I not change my delivery country online?

The subscription price includes the delivery charges, which varies from country to country. We therefore may have to adjust the amount of issues remaining on your subscription to reflect this. Our customer services agents can help you with this and can make sure your subscription is sent to your new address without any interruption.

Delivery problems or missing issues/gifts
Where’s my magazine?

Please allow up to eight weeks for delivery of the first subscription issue for both UK and Overseas orders. If you want to know when your next issue will arrive, you can check this by logging into your account and selecting ‘View full schedule’.

If you’ve missed an issue, you can request a replacement by logging into your account. If the problem persists, in the first instance check your details by logging into your account – if all is correct, please raise a support query below.

Where’s my welcome gift?

If your new subscription came with a gift, it will be sent to you within 28 days after we have collected the first payment from your account. Please note that gifts aren’t available for overseas subscribers.

Where’s my gift card?

If you have set up an ecard via our magazinesdirect.com website and the email has not been received, please ask your friend to check their junk/spam folder.

You can also request a replacement (sent either via post or to be downloaded and printed) by logging into your account or filling out the form below.

Accessing your subscription / logging in
Which email address did I register with?

This is likely to be the one used when purchasing the subscription. If someone bought the subscription for you as a gift, we might not have an email for you. We can still set you up with an account with a bit more information. Please type in the email address you’d like to use to get started.

I’ve just bought a new subscription and can’t login

Unfortunately, it can take up to 48 hours for a new subscription to be set up. If you ordered more than 48 hours ago and you are still having trouble logging in, please fill in the form below.

Only one of my subscriptions is showing up in my account

It can take up to 48 hours for a new subscription to be set up. If you ordered more than 48 hours ago and the subscription still isn’t showing on your account, please fill in the form below.

Payments and billing
How does Direct Debit work? What are its benefits?

UK customers have the option to subscribe by Direct Debit, which often offer the best deal. Depending on the magazine, your payments will be spread into easy, manageable 3, 6 or 12 monthly payments. As Direct Debit is a continuous payment method you are in complete control of your subscription as well as eliminating the need for renewal notices, making it the greener way to subscribe.

How does a ‘continuous credit card’ work?

Some promotions offer automatic renewal, paid for by debit or credit card. Before the start of each new term, we’ll simply charge your credit/debit card with the agreed payment amount. If there are any changes to the payment amount, we will send you a notification of this before we charge your card showing the new subscriber rate at that time, with a number to call and details of the online cancellation facility should you wish to cancel. As this is a continuous payment method you are in complete control of your subscription as well as eliminating the need for renewal notices.

Renewing a subscription
When should I renew my subscription? Will I miss any issues?

If you renew your subscription before the date highlighted on the ‘renew your subscription’ page, your subscription will continue without interruption and you will not miss any issues. If you renew after this date we cannot guarantee this.

What is included in the renewal price?

Prices displayed include delivery costs and taxes (where applicable). Any discounts to the full annual subscription rate will also be explained.

How do I purchase another subscription?

The best way to purchase an additional subscription is to visit our sister website www.magazinesdirect.com, where you will find information about all our publications and browse the latest offers for new customers.

Back issues
How do I purchase a single issue or a back issue?

If you wish to purchase a back issue of a Time Inc (UK) magazine you may find what you are looking for via a 3rd party website MagsUK. Alternatively, please call +44 (0)1733 688 964. Lines open 8:00am - 6.00pm Monday to Saturday (UK time).

Cancel a subscription
How do I cancel my subscription?

If your subscription has a minimum term of 12 months, you can only cancel your subscription at the end of this term. At the end of this minimum term, and for any subscription that does not have a minimum term, you can cancel by logging in and following the onscreen links.
